Sibol — Privacy Policy

Sibol is published by HealSpace Psychological Clinic (Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines).
Effective date: 28 June 2026 · Last updated: 28 June 2026

The short version. Sibol is a private, offline-first wellness companion. Your mood entries, journal notes, and settings are stored only on your own device. We do not run accounts, we do not collect or upload your data to our servers, and we do not use advertising or analytics trackers. Sibol is not a crisis or emergency service.

1. Who we are

This app, “Sibol” (the “App”), is provided by HealSpace Psychological Clinic (“HealSpace”, “we”, “us”). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@healspacelipa.com or visit healspacelipa.com.

2. What information the App handles

Sibol is designed to work fully offline. The information you create in the App is stored locally on your device:

  • Wellness content you enter — mood check-ins, emotions, journal notes, and progress data.
  • App preferences — settings such as reminders, accessibility options, and onboarding state.

This information is saved in your device’s local storage. Sensitive entries are kept using your device’s encrypted secure storage. None of it is transmitted to HealSpace or to any third party by the App.

3. Information we do not collect

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location through the App.
  • We do not use advertising, analytics, tracking, or profiling SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your information. Because your entries never leave your device, we have no copy of them to share.

4. Reminders and notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated on the device itself and are not sent through our servers. You can turn them off at any time in the App or in your device’s notification settings.

5. Contacting the clinic from within the App

The App provides buttons to reach HealSpace through external services such as Facebook Messenger, Viber, Instagram, WhatsApp, phone, email, or Google Maps. When you tap one of these, your device opens that separate app or website. Anything you then share is handled by that service under its own privacy policy, not ours.

6. Children’s privacy

Sibol is intended for general wellness use and is not directed at children under 13. Because the App does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly gather data from children. Minors should use the App with the guidance of a parent, guardian, or licensed professional.

7. Your control over your data

You stay in control of everything stored on your device:

  • Delete individual entries within the App where that option is available.
  • Clear all App data, or uninstall the App, to permanently remove the locally stored information from your device.

We cannot retrieve or restore this data for you, because we never receive a copy of it.

8. Security

We use your device’s standard secure-storage mechanisms to protect sensitive entries. No method of electronic storage is ever 100% secure, but keeping your data on-device (rather than on a server) significantly reduces risk. Please also protect your device with a screen lock.

9. Not a crisis or emergency service

Sibol supports everyday mental wellness and is not a substitute for professional care, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a 24/7 crisis service. If you or someone you know is in crisis, contact the Philippines’ National Center for Mental Health (NCMH) Crisis Hotline at 1553, or local emergency services at 911.

10. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and post the new version at this URL.
